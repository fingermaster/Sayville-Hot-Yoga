HOT YOGA CLASSES

Be prepared to sweat.
Bring a towel and drink plenty of water BEFORE attending.

HOT YOGA

This is our beginner Hot Yoga class, perfect for all levels and taught in a moist, heated room, which helps loosen tight muscles and promotes sweating to help detoxify the body. Foundational breathing techniques and alignment principles are taught, slowly and compassionately, with detailed instruction. Variations are offered to accomodate students that might find something too difficult, as well as to more advanced students that want more of a challenge. This class is transformational, will create radiant health, and build flexibility and strength for your body mind and spirit.

HOT VINYASA

Vinyasa classes move at a more energetic pace where students will learn to flow from one posture into the next in conjunction with the breath. Sun salutations along with a variety of postures and sequences, wraps/bindes, and inversions are taught. Ideal for advanced beginners ready for more of a challenge.

POWER YOGA

Power Yoga is our advanced class, incorporating more athletic and vigourous Vinyasa sequences with a large focus on inversions such as head stand, forearm stand and handstand.


NON-HEATED CLASSES

BEGINNER

Our Beginner Basics class is great for students new to yoga as well as seasoned practitioners seeking gentler, less challenging class. Students are taught basic poses, breathing and meditation techniques to help keep their body and mind healthy and prepare them for more advanced practices. Bring your family and friends and share the healing benefits of yoga together.

ASHTANGA

Ashtanga is a more athletic and powerful system of yoga designed to build strength, flexibility and stamina. The set series opens and detoxifies each part of the body, cleansing muscles and internal organs as well as improving circulation and overall health. This class is a perfect way to learn the fundamentals of Vinyasa as well as building a self practice at home. Students are urged to memorize the series enabling them to move into meditation and to build a deeper, more personal, self-practice.

RESTORATIVE

First Sunday of every month, from 12:30-2pm

Restorative Yoga is offered on the first Sunday of each month, from 12:30-2pm. This is a deeply relaxing yoga class, where postures are supported by props (pillows, blankets) enabling the student to fully relax and meditate, bringing the nervous system and spirit back to rest.

KUNDALINI

Monday's at 6pm

Awaken your chakras and heighten your spirits! Kundalini Yoga is the yoga of awareness, aiming to cultivate the creative spiritual potential of a human to uphold values, speak truth, and focus on the compassion and consciousness needed to serve and heal others.

KIDS YOGA

Kids Yoga classes are intended for children ages 5-9, and run in conjunction with our Tuesday and Thursday 4pm, and Saturday 10am Beginner Hot Yoga classes, enabling parents to attend class while their children are in session. *Pre-registeration is a MUST for the Tuesday and Thursday 4pm Kids Yoga, please call by 12 noon to sign your child up. Schedule is subject to change, please check the schedule online before attending to make sure Kids Yoga classes are in session.

DIPAMKARA MEDITATION

Every Sunday, 7pm-8:30pm with Buddhist teacher Todd Drexler

Eight Steps to Happiness by Geshe Kelsang Gyatso explains eight beautiful verses that comprise one of Buddhism's best-loved and most enduring teachings. This short text reveals profound yet totally practical methods to enable a powerful awakening of the heart, the one true source of all of our happiness. Drop in to any class. $5 SHY members $10 non-members.
