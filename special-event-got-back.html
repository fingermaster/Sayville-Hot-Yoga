Classical Yoga and Satsang (Discourse) with
Special Guest Yogi Prakesh Dhungana from Nepal


Every Sunday in February, 12-2pm

Join Yogi Prakash all the way from Nepal for All Level practical yoga classes. Experience how the effects of asana can be magnified when combined with pranayama, mudra, mantra and meditation. Yogi Prakash is here to bring the full depth of yoga from the land where it originated.

Following the asana practice, stay for an open discussion on Tantra and Yoga. Yogi Prakesh will speak about the true nature of Tantra and its connection with yoga. Come with an open mind and any questions you have had about either.

Classes are $10 for SHY Members. Or $20 Drop In, Non-Members.
All proceeds will go to support Lotus Project - Shanti Yoga Ashram Nepal,
a non-profit organization that provides academic and yogic education to underprivileged children in Nepal.

To learn more about Yogi Prakash please visit Shanti Yoga Ashram
